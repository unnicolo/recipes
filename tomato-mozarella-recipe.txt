Ingredients:
 - 1 portion of Galbani mozarella
 - 2 tomatoes
 - extra vergine olive oil
 - balsamico vinegar
 - salt
 - pepper
 - basil leaves

Directions:
 1. Cut the mozarella and the tomatoes into slices.
 2. Place the mozarella slices onto a plate and slightly drizzle with balsamico
    vinegar. Cover the mozarella slices with the tomatoes. Put the oil and a
    little more of the vinegar onto the salat (careful with the vinegar!).
 3. Season with salt and pepper and decorate with basil leaves.
